Services

Our model is centred on technology and sustainability and is designed to ensure that every commodity coming in and going out of any of our warehouses or from our community universally accepted and synonymous with quality.To guaranty this, we offer the following services to our community

Agricultural Produce Certification

We support our clients with a complete range of testing, inspection and certification services to provide agri-commodities businesses with the confidence to trade

Visual inspections

At your convenience, our qualified personnel can assess and certify your agricultural produce. This seal ensures that your goods can be sold atlocal or international markets at competitive prices. We provide our community with packaging requirements and check all produce for consistency and quality. Our tracking systems provide farm to fork tracking mechanisms which provide can help track contaminated food.

Testing

Farm Warehouse performs accurate testing services with independence and integrity. Among our available services are bacteriological and chemical sampling analyses and quality analyses of produce samples.

Agricultural Loans

Farm Warehouse provides access to third party agricultural loans and guarantees to increase the availability of loans to farmers and agricultural co-operatives.

Farmers can use these loans to establish, improve, and develop farms, while agricultural co-operatives may also access loans to process, distribute, or market the products of farming.

Farmers in our community can contact Farm Warehouse information desk directly to inquire about loans that they are qualified for.
