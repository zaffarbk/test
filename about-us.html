ABOUT US

Farm Warehouse was borne out of a vision to provide value to small holder farmers and off-takers in the agricultural value chain. From micro credits, to insurance, to compact scalable processing equipment and warehousing facilities, we eliminate the inefficiencies that exist in the sector and strive to make agriculture more sustainable. Our and e-commerce platform ensures that our community can access a variety of commodities, inputs, equipment at the best prices.

Purpose

Farm Warehouse looks to provide value, security and transparency to every transaction on our network.

Working with Chainlink Oracles we seek to revolutionize the process of agri-business by creating an interconnected global economy supported by reliable real-time data to empower millions of smallholder farmers and off takersthrough the provision of micro credits, insurance options, produce certification, agri-extension services, agricultural pre-processing equipment and warehousing facilities.

Our system ensures that usersobtain the true value of their transactions and eliminates the additional costs of traditional methods. In addition, our system would guaranty financial inclusion for the most vulnerable in the society and improve food security.

Technology

Farm Warehouse leverages the security, decentralized nature, speed of transacting, transparency and immutability of blockchain technology to deliver value in the agri-business space.

We utilize the Avalanche blockchain with Chainlink oracles technology to process transactions and deliver value to our customers.

Our commodity processing/storage hubs and farmer registration centres are powered by clean renewable energy -creating a system that leaves zero carbon footprints from the beginning of our process to the end. Our approach proves that we can make significant impacts and still be environmentally sustainable.
