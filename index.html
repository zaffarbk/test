Virtual Solutions for Agriculture

We have developed an initiative for the agricultural value chain called the FAMWEC (Farmers Aggregated Market for Wealth and Enterprise Cultivation) Initiative.

This is a systematic solution is aimed at solving the agricultural commodity demand and supply challenge by providing a virtual market and warehouse for agricultural commodities.

We aggregate smallholder farmers’ demand for inputs and obtain favourable prices from suppliers. Through our network of logistics and delivery partners, farm produce aggregated from farmers registered under the FAMWEC programme can be conveyed to fulfilment centres and accessed by our off takers.

Benifits

By providing a structured platform for our usersto market their produce and commodities, we eliminate post-harvest losses from the farmers, provideaccess to micro-credits and agricultural & health Insurance for the farmers and provide certified quality produce from the farmers to off-takers. This creates a myriad of opportunitiesfor wealth and enterprise in the agriculture value chain.

Post-harvest Loss reduction

Our processing hubs and warehousing facilities enable smallholder farmers eliminate yield losses from poor post-harvest practices..

Access to Credit

Our system facilitates access to working capital credits. Awarded credits can be used to procure production inputs such as seedlings, fertilizers and pesticides. It can also afford the farmers mechanized farming services for increased efficiency.

This means that smallholder farmers are equipped with sufficient working capital through the farming season and they can sell their produceat good prices after harvest to repay the credit.
